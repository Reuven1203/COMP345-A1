This project adheres to specific d20 game rules across its various components. Here is a brief overview\+:\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md8}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Abilities Assignment Based on Equipped Items}{Abilities Assignment Based on Equipped Items}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md8}
Characters have their abilities (e.\+g., Strength, Dexterity) and stats (e.\+g., Armor Class) dynamically adjusted based on the items they equip. Each item type (e.\+g., Helmet, Armor) has specific effects on the character\textquotesingle{}s abilities and stats, determined by enchantment modifiers. For example, equipping a Helmet might increase a character\textquotesingle{}s Intelligence and Wisdom, while a Belt might boost Strength and Constitution. This dynamic adjustment allows for a flexible and strategic approach to character development and gameplay, adhering to the d20 system\textquotesingle{}s rules.
